How we describe risk

There are 4 different levels of risk for flooding from:

  • flash flooding (surface water)
  • rivers
  • the sea
Risk level Likelihood of flooding
High Greater than 3.3%
Medium Between 1% and 3.3%
Low Between 0.1% and 1%
Very Low Less than 0.1%

For groundwater and reservoirs, we will just tell you if there is a risk.

Facts about flooding

In England and Wales:

  • 5.9 million properties (1 in 6 homes) are at risk of flooding
  • £28,000 is the average cost of a home flooding

Who flooding affects

Anybody can be affected by flooding, iuncluding people who live:

  • in a top floor flatl
  • on a hill
  • far from a body of water like a river or stream

    • The impact of flooding

    • access to clean water and food supplies
    • gas, electricity and drainage
    • doctor’s surgery or hospital
    • transport - cars, buses and trains

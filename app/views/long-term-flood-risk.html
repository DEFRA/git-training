How we describe risk

This is how we describe the risk for flash flooding (surface water), rivers and the sea

Risk Level Chance each year
High Greater than 3.3%
Medium Between 1% and 3.3%
Low Between 0.1% and 1%
Very Low Less than 0.1%

We will just tell you if there is a risk for groundwater and reservoirs.

In England and Wales

For groundwater and reservoirs, we will just tell you if there is a risk.

In England and Wales

  • 5.9 million properties are at risk of flooding - 1 in 6 homes
  • £28,000 - the average cost of a home flooding

Anybody can be affected by flooding - even if you live in a top floor flat.

Read about the impacts of flooding

You can still sufer from flooding, even if you live:

  • on a hill
  • far from a body of water like a river or stream
It can impact your:
  • health - through disease or impact on mental health
  • access to clean water and food supplies
  • gas, electricity and drainage
  • doctor’s surgery or hospital
  • transport - cars, buses and trains
  • school, place of work, community centre or place of worship
